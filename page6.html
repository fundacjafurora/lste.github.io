DAY 2

Second day was full of engaging and interactive activities designed to enhance our teamwork, communication, creativity, and problem-solving skills. The working day began with an energizer prepared by a responsible group, which helped us start the day with a positive attitude and high energy. The first working session focused on group discussion about our fears, expectations, and contributions to the project we participate in. This session enabled us to share our thoughts, get to know each other better, and establish a foundation of trust, which is essential for effective teamwork.

The second session was called "Mission Impossible," and it featured several creative activities designed to integrate the group. We posted our crazy selfies, group photos, and song playlists, which helped us get to know each other's interests and personalities better.
After lunch, we engaged in a session on debating and negotiating, where we read a story about five people and discussed ranking the characters from the most to the least guilty in the story. We began the discussion in pairs and then moved on to finding final solutions for the whole group. This session taught us essential aspects of communication, mediation, and democracy.

The last session of the day involved discovering our personality creativity type based on a system that divided it into eight different types. After learning the basics about our creativity types, we worked in groups containing people based on their creativity type to brainstorm business ideas or improve existing businesses. We discussed what to improve in the situation and how to create a new strategy for a business, which was an excellent exercise for problem-solving and teamwork.
In conclusion, today activities provided an enriching experience that helped us develop crucial skills for teamwork, communication, creativity, and problem- solving. The activities were interactive and engaging, and the trainers were knowledgeable and supportive throughout the day. We look forward to applying the skills we have learned to our projects and personal lives.

~ WOJCIECH
