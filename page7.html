09.05.2023

DAY 1

Today was the beginning of our project, during which participants had the opportunity to get to know each other. We all gathered in a pleasant and modern environment, ready to collaborate and create something extraordinary.
After the warm welcome, each of us introduced ourselves, exchanging our names. It was a nice and friendly moment where we started building the initial connections. We quickly realized that the group is diverse in terms of age, experience, and professional backgrounds. Such diversity is undoubtedly an asset that will help us look at our project from different perspectives.
After the formalities, it was time for the main part of the day - sharing our passions. Each of us had a few minutes to talk about what we love to do in our free time and what we are passionate about. It was truly inspiring to hear about the variety of interests and talents within our group.
We also focused on understanding the goals and principles of the Erasmus+ program and identifying opportunities for its implementation in our project. Working in groups allowed us to exchange perspectives and ideas, contributing to a better understanding and exploration of the potential that these programs can offer. During our discussions, we also addressed the Erasmus for Entrepreneurs program, which provides an opportunity for experience exchange and collaboration among entrepreneurs in Europe. We explored different actions within this program and discussed the benefits it can bring to emerging businesses. The insights from our discussions were inspiring and added extra motivation to continue the project. We are ready to make use of the opportunities provided by the Erasmus+ and Erasmus for Entrepreneurs programs to collectively create something valuable and positive for our community.

~ NATALIA
