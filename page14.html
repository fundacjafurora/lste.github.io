DAY 9

The ninth and final day of the project marked a significant milestone as we reached the culmination of our efforts. The day began with the participants dedicating their time to the final refinement of their project ideas. Teams worked diligently to incorporate the knowledge and insights gained throughout the program, refining their business plans, marketing strategies, and financial projections. The goal was to create a comprehensive and well-rounded presentation that showcased the potential of their projects. It was a period of intense collaboration and fine-tuning, as teams aimed to present their ideas in the best possible light.

After the project refinement phase, we proceeded to the simulation activity. Participants assumed the role of investors and were tasked with allocating a predetermined sum of money to the projects they believed had the highest potential for success. This exercise challenged participants to analyze and assess various projects based on their viability, market potential, and sustainability. It was an engaging and competitive activity that allowed us to understand the investor's perspective and sharpen our decision-making skills.

In the second part of the day, we conducted project evaluation. and we summarized the overall project experience. We reflected on the knowledge gained, skills acquired, and the personal growth we experienced throughout the program. Participants shared their key takeaways, highlighting the most valuable aspects of the project and the impact it had on their entrepreneurial mindset. It was a moment of celebration and gratitude for the collective journey we embarked upon together.

The day concluded with the distribution of YouthPass certificates. Each participant received a certificate recognizing their active participation and contribution to the project. It was a symbolic moment that acknowledged our dedication, hard work, and commitment to personal and professional development.

As we wrapped up the final day, there was a sense of accomplishment and fulfillment in the air. The journey we embarked upon together over the past nine days had been transformative, inspiring us to pursue our entrepreneurial dreams with newfound confidence and knowledge. We expressed our gratitude to the organizers, facilitators, and fellow participants for their support and collaboration throughout the project.


~ STEFAN
