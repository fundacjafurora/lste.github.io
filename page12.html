15.05.2023

DAY 7

The day commenced with our customary energizers, designed to invigorate and motivate everyone for the tasks ahead. The team gathered, participating in lively activities and exercises to awaken our senses and foster a sense of camaraderie.

Following the energizers, we delved into the heart of our projects. Our primary focus was on creating project logos that encapsulated the essence of our respective endeavors. Each team member poured their creativity into designing a visually appealing and meaningful logo that would resonate with our target audience. Simultaneously, we dedicated time to refining our business development plan, ensuring it was comprehensive and aligned with our objectives.

Once the lunch break arrived, we reconvened to showcase our project logos and present our business development plans to the rest of the team. This was an opportunity for us to receive constructive feedback, suggestions, and fresh perspectives. As each team member presented their ideas, we engaged in productive discussions, refining and improving our strategies. The collective expertise and diverse viewpoints brought immense value to the table, enriching our understanding and pushing us to explore new avenues. To infuse a touch of creativity and innovation, we decided to present our business ideas in the form of theater. Collaboratively, we crafted scripts, designed sets, and honed our acting skills. The stage transformed into a platform where we showcased our concepts, not merely through traditional presentations, but through engaging performances that allowed us to connect with the emotions and imagination of our audience.

The theater presentations were met with enthusiasm and applause. The dynamic nature of the performances brought our ideas to life, allowing the audience to grasp the vision and potential behind each project. Theatrical elements, such as dramatic narratives, compelling dialogues, and expressive acting, enhanced the impact of our presentations and made them memorable experiences for everyone involved. As the day drew to a close, we shifted our focus towards preparing for the next day's activities. We organized our materials, reviewed the schedule, and ensured that all necessary arrangements were in place. By wrapping up the day with meticulous planning, we aimed to start the following day on a smooth and productive note, ready to tackle new challenges and continue our journey towards success.

In summary, the day encompassed energizing activities, logo creation, business plan presentations, and theater performances. It was a day of collaborative creativity, where ideas flourished, feedback was shared, and our projects were brought to life through the power of theater. The day's activities not only strengthened our project plans but also fostered a sense of unity and excitement within the team. With preparations made for the next day, we concluded the day with a sense of accomplishment and anticipation for the opportunities that lie ahead.

On the seventh day of the project, we also had the opportunity to experience a cultural evening, where each country represented in our group showcased their local traditions, customs, and cuisine. It was a delightful and engaging event that allowed us to appreciate the rich diversity and cultural heritage of our international team.


~ YERASSYL
